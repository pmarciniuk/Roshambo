Rules

The idea is that both players have an equal chance of winning, making the game random but fair.

A person who decides to play rock will beat another player who has chosen scissors ("rock crushes scissors"), but will lose to one who has played paper ("paper covers rock"); a play of paper will lose to a play of scissors ("scissors cut[s] paper").

Good luck!
